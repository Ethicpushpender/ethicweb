POWER OF HABITS


Event- POWER OF HABITS
Date – 5th August 2019
Venue – IMS Engineering College, Ghaziabad.

Ethiccraft Club conducted an orientation program at IMS Engineering College, Ghaziabad on 5th August 2019. The orientation program comprised of a seminar on the topic" Power of Habits" and a small presentation on the upcoming Mega Youth Festival Udgaar. The seminar was wonderfully presented by Mr Anuj Saxena, Advocate Supreme Court of India. The seminar was attended by a total of more than 250 students and many students expressed their interest in registration for Udgaar festival.
