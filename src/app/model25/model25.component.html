ORIENTATION PROGRAM AT DPGITM, GURUGRAM


Event- ORIENTATION PROGRAM AT DPGITM
Date – 10th October, 2019
Venue – Seminar Hall, DPGITM.

Ethiccraft Club organized a seminar for the first year students of BBA, B.Tech and BCA in the Induction Program of DPG ITM College, Sec-34, Gurgaon. The Guest Speaker, Mr. Ankit Malik,who is M.Tech (Gold Medalist), IIT BHU, delivered an enlightening and a thoughtful talk on "Life Apps" Around 150 students attended the 45 min talk. Mr. Ankit related the contemporary issues and problems and linked their root causes and solutions to the wisdom of the timeless scriptures. Topics such as empowerment without education, responsibility of youth were enunciated in great detail and appreciated by everyone listening The students enjoyed the not so common practical knowledge imparted and many wrote of organizing such programs in the course of their studies to help them in their holistic development along with subject education.
