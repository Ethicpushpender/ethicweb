CAMPUS AMBASSADOR TRIP TO KURUKSHETRA


Date – 13TH APRIL 2019
Venue – Kurukshetra.
Places Visit: Brahm Sarovar, Panorama, Sri Krishna Museum, Jyotisar.

“Wisdom comes not by simply resting in the rocking chair and pondering over things. It comes by stepping out of your comfort zone, into the unexplored dimensions of the outer world…”

In a gesture of gratitude and acknowledgement of the valuable endeavours and efforts of all the Campus Ambassadors towards the success of Anti-Addiction Quiz Campaign in their respective colleges, Ethiccraft Club conducted a one day recreational and educational camp to Kurukshetra, the city with an ancient connection to battle with the unrighteous forces prevalent in the society.

It is truly said that “When we travel, we unravel – new places, new people, and a new own self”. The Kurukshetra Camp, organized on 13th April 2019, was a baggage of amazement for every inquisitive individual. The trip started from Delhi when Campus Ambassadors, the torch-bearers of colleges boarded the trip bus. After a 3 hour journey, the arrival at the land of wisdom delighted everyone. This was followed by an enlightening talk by HG Sundar Gopal Das, President Ethiccraft Club and HG Ashray Krishna Das, Senior Faculty Member Ethiccraft. After this, the participants took bath in Brahm Sarovar which appeared like an entrance into the tranquillity. Kurukshetra Panorama Centre, Science Museum, and Sri Krishna Museum provided an enriching, informative experience. After this, HG Sundar Gopal Das and HG Ashray Krishna Das addressed everyone about upcoming activities and its vision. This was followed by an aesthetic visit to Jyotisar, the origins of the Bhagvad Gita.
