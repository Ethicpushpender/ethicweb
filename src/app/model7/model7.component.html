SEMINAR ON TIME MANAGEMENT


Event- SEMINAR ON TIME MANAGEMENT
Date – 23rd January 2019
Venue – Seminar Hall, DPGITM College Gurgaon.

“Once a moment of time is lost, it cannot be regained even by the wealth of the whole world” quoted Chanakaya Pandit which helps one understand the value of time. In an endeavour to help the students know the valuable principles associated with Time Management, Ethiccraft Club conducted a riveting seminar on Time Management at DPG Institute of Technology and Management, Gurgaon on 23rd January 2019. The seminar aimed to let the students know various aspects of time management including the art of scheduling, monitoring and priority setting. The seminar was very eloquently delivered by Mr Shivanshu Singh, Vice-President, Ethiccraft Club who covered the topic through 5 practical activities. The seminar was attended by more than 220 students.
