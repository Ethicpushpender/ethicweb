POWER OF HABIT


Event- POWER OF HABIT
Date – 9th September, 2019
Venue – NITRA, Ghaziabad.

Taking a step forward towards fulfilling the Club motto" Marching towards Values", Ethiccraft Club organized a seminar on "Power of Habits" at NITRA, Ghaziabad on 9th September, 2019. The students of the institution were blessed with words of wisdom by Mr Robin Batra Sir, Co-founder JBS Technologies and faculty member, Ethiccraft. The whole program was coordinated by RKGIT Ethiccraft team.
