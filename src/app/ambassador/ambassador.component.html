As part of spreading its youths outreach, Ethiccraft Club wishes to create campus ambassadors for the colleges. The Value Education Ambassadors is a leadership opportunity for students who are dedicated to serving and representing Ethiccraft Club in college. Value Education Ambassadors serve as a club representative inside the College and is expected to lead and perform duties at various campus and community events of the institution.

Value EDucation AMbassadors(VEDAM) Responsibilities

  • Represent and lead the Club during college events.
  • Head the meetings of the club members inside the college.
  • Inspire the club members in the college for leadership roles.
  • Prepare activities chart of the club for the college.
  • Take initiative for conducting various events inside the college.

VEDAM Membership Standards

1. Currently Enrolled College Student- The student must be a currently enrolled student of the college and be an active student of the college.

2. Strong Academics- The VEDAM must possess strong academic background with excellence in education as well as extra-curricular activities.

3. Position Commitment-The position of VEDAM has a one academic year commitment. Student Ambassador is expected to continue with the responsibility for at least one full academic year.
