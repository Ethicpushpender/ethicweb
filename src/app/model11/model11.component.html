UDGAAR VALUE AWARENESS QUIZ


Event- UDGAAR VALUE AWARENESS QUIZ
Date – 14 Oct 2018
Venue – Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium.

From the dawn of human civilization, the importance of Value Education has been realized by pioneers and thinkers all over the world. Value Education helps in empowering people towards leading principle centered life. Value Education is also necessary in order to develop the core human values like integrity, honesty, truthfulness, trustworthiness, etc. These are the foundational principles for a peaceful and prosperous society. Though human civilization has made tremendous advancement in the field of material education in developing various facilities for making life comfortable. However, recent incidents of depression, suicides, drug abuse, unhealthy addictions, crimes, corruption, etc. clearly indicates that there is decline in basic human values. There are diverse views and opinions available on the topic. But, some concrete initiative on ground level is required in order to make significant progress in this direction.
To achieve the same, IYF came with the idea of Udgaar Festival Value Awareness Quiz in order to create awareness about the values and ethics among youths of the country. The quiz was organized in 21 colleges of Delhi-NCR in which a total of around 1000 youths participated. The quiz was conducted at two levels- Intra-College Quiz and Inter-College Quiz. Among the 21 colleges who participated in Intra-College Quiz, top 6 colleges were invited for the Inter-College Value Awareness Quiz at Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium during Udgaar Festival. The youths who participated in this Value Awareness Program are the one who will lead India and build India upon values.
