GET INVOLVED

Albert Einstein Said, ’Life is like riding a bicycle; to keep the balance one has to keep moving.’ For most people, there is no time to stop to learn how to maintain good relationships, how to become effective leaders, how to manage complications in life, and so on and so forth. They are always on the bicycle of life. Thus, they actually go through life while caring very little for growing through life. Growing through life happens by learning and this learning facilitates the holistic growth of the individual and society. Thus, any effort to help retain the balance is always welcome.

In this direction, the role of youths is of topmost importance. Youths possesses that energy and potential to work out an extra edge as compared to other age groups. This gives leaders a great opportunity to effectively utilize young lads to empower country towards success. This opportunity can be capitalized if the powers of the youth is harnessed properly. Just as when the atomic energy is harnessed properly, it can resolve all energy related issues within the country. Similarly, if the youth energy is harnessed properly we can’t even imagine the outcome it can bring. Therefore, it is very important to realize the youth power and utilize it effectively. All we need is to direct the energy of our youth in constructive channels that lead to development and progress.

As an initiative towards youth development and empowerment, Ethiccraft club offers membership opportunities to talented and energetic youths of the country. Come and become instrumental in exhibiting that enthusiasm and determination which may script the dawn of new era filled with constructive, peace-loving and selfless personalities and make the world a beautiful peace to live..'

Benefits of Club Membership:

1.Awards and Certificates — The members of the club would be felicitated with awards and certificates for their contribution in the club activities. The students can use these certificates with their resumes or curriculum vitae.

2.Big Scale Recognition — Special performers of the club would get an opportunity to be rewarded in upcoming Youth Festival by President/ Prime Minister and other dignitaries.

3.Leadership Training — As an individual, a youth will get an opportunity to get trained in leadership roles which will help one embark on the path of success.

4.Interaction with eminent personalities — The membership will also bless a member to get learning opportunities from eminent and successful personalities all across the globe and grow oneself.

5.Leadership in college/universities — The member would emerge as the leader in his institution due to his involvement with the club.

6.Opportunity for Contribution — The Club aims to provide its members an opportunity to contribute towards social and national development and experience a sense of self-fulfilment by helping and serving people in need.

Member

Ethiccraft members are the youths who are connected with the club activities at some level. These may include youths from colleges, coaching or professional background. Members of Ethiccraft Club are expected to volunteer for the different programs conducted by club in order to ensure smooth conduct events

Campus Ambassador

As part of spreading its youths outreach, Ethiccraft Club wishes to create campus ambassadors for the colleges. The Value Education Ambassadors is a leadership opportunity for students who are dedicated to serving and representing Ethiccraft Club in college.

Ethiccraft Executive Officer

Execution Committee is meant to prepare the layout and structure of the overall planning and ensure the execution of policies at ground level. Execution Committee with a team of thoughtful and selfless youths is expected to help the college volunteers of Ethiccraft Club
