7 SESSION SEMINAR SERIES


Event- 7 SESSION SEMINAR SERIES
Date – 28th January to 2nd February
Venue – AV Room, Bhaskaracharya College.

With the motto” Marching towards Values”, Ethiccraft Club conducted a 7 seminar series at the Bhaskaracharya College of Applied Sciences. The event, conducted by Ethiccraft Club in collaboration with Bhaskaracharya Cell on the occasion of Bhaskaracharya week from 28th January to 2nd February 2019 was a step-forward towards imparting values among youth. On the first day, HG Sundar Gopal Das, President Ethiccraft enlightened the students on the topic” Extracts from Life of Bhaskaracharya.” In the second half, Mr Shivanshu Singh, Vice-President Ethiccraft shed light on the topic”Ethics from Epics.” On 29th January, HG Amogha Lila Das, Vice_President ISKCON Dwarka delivered a mesmerizing seminar on ” Unravelling the Glorious Ancient Indian Education. This was followed by a knowledge enriching seminar on topic” Secrets of Success” by Mr Abhinav Grover, Assistant professor JNU.

While the intellectual journey was becoming more and more interesting, the next seminar on the topic” Science and Spirituality” was delivered by Mr Deepak Meena, alumni IIT Delhi. On 1st Feb, students got an opportunity to hear from HG Aravindakasha Madhav das on the topic” Life on Two Wheels.” on the last day of 7 seminar series, HG Sundar Gopal Das delivered the seminar on the topic”Wake Up Indian! Discover Your Glories.” This was followed by a presentation on Ethiccraft Club, its mission, its event and upcoming events especially the De-addiction Campaign starting from 8th February 2019.

This initiative by Ethiccraft Club in the college premises will surely help the students of the college to become empowered with wisdom. The event earned lots of appreciation from students and the testimonial of fact is that more than 120 students attended the seminar series.
