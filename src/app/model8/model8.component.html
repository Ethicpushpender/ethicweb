LEADERSHIP SEMINAR


Event- LEADERSHIP SEMINAR
Date – 04TH Jan 2019
Venue – Jawaharlal Nehru University.

With the objective of helping the youth learn and cultivate the leadership traits and qualities, a seminar on the topic”Developing the Culture of Leadership” was organized In Jawahar Lal university by Ethiccraft Club in collaboration with the placement cell of the university. The seminar very elaborately presented the various concepts associated with Leadership which are very relevant and practical for the emerging leaders of the nation. The session was delivered by Mr. Shubha Vilas, author of many National Best Selling books including Ramayana-The Game of Life, Open Eyed Meditation etc. He is also an inspirational orator, TEDx speaker, motivational speaker and a lifestyle coach and the students felt very benefited by his association.
