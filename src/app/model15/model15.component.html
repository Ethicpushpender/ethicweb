ORIENTATION PROGRAM


Event- ORIENTATION PROGRAM
Date – 21st August 2019
Venue – Daulat Ram Girls College, Delhi University.

On 21st August , a seminar was organized by Ethiccraft club at Daulat Ram Girls College, University of Delhi on the topic “ Power of habits”. Event saw a great footfall of enthusiastic students which filled the whole auditorium with air of awe. Event stared with a small story of watermelon seller putting smiles on faces of students. Many topics of great social interests were touched by the Honorable Vice president of Ethiccraft Club Mr. Shivanshu Singh. Students were made aware of how people fall in trap of big company’s propaganda which support evils like alcohol and cigarettes and how these become habits if not dealt with strong intelligence. Event culminated with thunderous applause marking success of the event. The volunteers registered many students for Quiz and appreciated them for their understanding and support for this noble cause.
