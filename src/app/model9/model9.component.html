YOUTH PROGRAM AND SEMINAR AT RKGIT


Event- YOUTH PROGRAM AND SEMINAR AT RKGIT SEMINAR, GHAZIABAD
Date – 20 Nov 2018
Venue – Auditorium .

YOUTH PROGRAM AND SEMINAR AT RKGIT, GHAZIABAD 20 Nov 2018 admin Recent Comments Off on Youth Program and Seminar at RKGIT, Ghaziabad Ethiccraft Club, in a step forward towards helping the youths know the various important values of human life and cultivate the natural goodness of the heart conducted a seminar for the students of Raj Kumar Goel Institute of Technology (RKGIT) Ghaziabad. In the initial part of the program, Prof. H.N. Garg awarded a warm welcome to the Ethiccraft Team and expressed his gratitude to the members for taking out their valuable time. After an introduction, Ethiccraft Club Vice President enlightened the students about the vision and purpose of the Club. This was followed by a thoughtful and fascinating seminar on the topic” Pleasure Hunt” which was attended by a total of more than 200 students. The session was delivered very eloquently by Mr. Anuj Saxena, Advocate, Supreme Court of India. The students got an opportunity to learn some very important principles pertaining their lives. After the program, the students felt inspired to attend many more events to be conducted by club in future.
