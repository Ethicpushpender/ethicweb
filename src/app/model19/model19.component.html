CONFERENCE ON STRESS MANAGEMENT


Event- CONFERENCE ON STRESS MANAGEMENT
Date – 18TH SEPTEMBER, 2019
Venue – DU Conference Hall.

Ethiccraft Club in association with Delhi University, very successfully organized a "Conference on Stress Management". The Conference included a talk by Adv. Anuj Saxena Sir and a talk by Honorable President Sir of Ethiccraft Club, HG Sundar Gopal Ji. This was followed by a panel discussion which included Dr Satish Gosain, Mr Ankit Aggrawal and Prof Diwakar as the panellist along with President Sir and Anuj Sir.

The conference was attended by a total of ,800 students. Dean of Students Welfare, DU was very happy with the program.
