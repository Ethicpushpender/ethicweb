UDGAAR(An Expression of Goodness & Joy)


Event- UDGAAR(An Expression of Goodness & Joy)
Date – 6th October, 2019
Venue – Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium.

Udgaar With great fervour and enthusiasm, much-awaited Annual Mega Youth Festival of Ethiccraft Club entitled” Udgaar: An Expression of Goodness and Joy” was organized at Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium on 6th October 2019. The theme of this year festival was ‘Addiction-Free India.” The event witnessed a participation of many prominent dignitaries including Dr. P.B. Sharma, VC-Amity University and Chindi Vasudevyappa, VC NIFTEM, Sonepat and many college directors and principals along with more than 12000 youth across the country. Dr. Harshvardhan, Health Minister, Govt of India was the Chief Guest on the occasion.
The event started with mesmerizing musical performance by Madhavas Rock Band, runner up of India’s Got Talent Season 3. This was followed by a dance performance on National Anthem by Prince Dance Group. In the next part of the festival, toppers of 6 colleges came to lock horns for an intellectual battle to prove their awareness on the subject of addictions through Inter-College Anti-addiction Quiz. This was followed by a welcome address by HH Loknath Swami Maharaj and inspirational messages by Dr. Rakesh kumar Chaddha, Chief-NDDTC, AIIMS and HG Anuttama Pr, Minister of ISKCON Communications. After that, a wonderful theatrical presentation named’ Stay High Forever” depicting the methodologies of dealing with addictions was presented. In the next part, Dr. Veerendra Mishra, Director NISD, HH Saccinandan Swami Maharaj and Mr R.K. Sinha, MP, Rajya Sabha presented a wholesome approach of dealing with addictions. In the next part, Feel Crew Dance group fascinated the audience with a splendid performance. Next was the felicitation of all guests and Inter-College quiz winners. This was followed by the presentation of World Book of Records Certificate by WBR officials marking this festival as the largest drug awareness lesson ever in the world history. After this, HH Gopal Krishna Goswami, GBC ISKCON and BBT Trustee delivered words of wisdom and Dr. Harshvardhan shared his realizations on drug addiction prevention. In the next part, Dr. Vivek Bindra, founder of Badabusiness.com enthralled the audience with his keynote speech. He delineated many key principles for holistic success. After this, sand art by Nitish Bharti and dance performance by Prince Group were very captivating. At last, the festival concluded with vote of thanks and a rocking performance by Madhavas Rock Band.
