ORIENTATION PROGRAM AT RKGIT


Event- ORIENTATION PROGRAM AT RKGIT
Date – 13th August, 2019
Venue – Raj Kumar Goel Institute of Technology, Ghaziabad.

On a special invitation from Raj Kumar Goel Institute of Technology, Ghaziabad, Ethiccraft Club organized an orientation program for the first year students of the institution on 13th August, 2019. The program was divided into two parts. In the first part, the students were blessed with wonderful jewels of wisdom by Anuj Saxena Sir, Advocate Supreme Court of India on the topic" Power of Habits." In the second part, Shivanshu Singh Sir, Vice-President of Ethiccraft Club shared wonderful methods and techniques in relation to time management and scheduling while addressing students on the topic" Art of Time Management." The program was also attended by Director Sir, Dean Sir and other senior faculty members of the institution.
