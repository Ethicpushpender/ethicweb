GANGA GHATS CLEANING & FESTIVAL OF LIGHT


Event- GANGA GHATS CLEANING & FESTIVAL OF LIGHT
Date – 20th October, 2019
Venue – Ganga Ghats, Patna.

On 20th October, NIT Patna Ethiccraft Club team successfully organized a festival full of Happiness, Love and Cleanliness named "Deep Utshav" at the bank of Holy Ganges. The volunteers of Ethiccraft came together with the mission of spreading goodness in the society and promote a sense of belongingness to the environment. After Cleaning Ganga Ghats and decorating it with Diya, the volunteers lit the light of Joy and Happiness. Team Ethiccraft would like to Congratulate all the members for their active participation in the event and making it a grand success.
