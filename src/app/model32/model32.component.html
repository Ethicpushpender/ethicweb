Viplava - Mega Youth Fest, 2023



DATE: 20th October, 2023
VENUE: Govardhan Hills, Uttar-Pradesh, India.

In a follow up program owf the conference on Stress Management at Delhi University, Ethiccraft Club organized one workshop on Stress Management Skills at Ram Lal Anand College, South Campus Delhi University on 22nd October, 2019. The workshop was delivered by Anuj Saxena Sir, Adv Supreme Court of India and Shivanshu Singh Sir, Vice-president, Ethiccraft. In the workshop, Anuj Sir delineated mentioned some valuable principles in dealing with stress and leading a happy life. In the second part, Shivanshu Sir told some practical tips for coping up with stress. Principal sir and Rita Jain madam, overall coordinator of the workshop also attended the whole program with some other faculty members.
