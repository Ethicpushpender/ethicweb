QUEST FOR HAPPINESS


Event- QUEST FOR HAPPINESS
Date – 20th August 2019
Venue – IMS Ghaziabad.

As part of spreading its outreach across management students who will become future business leaders, managers of the country, Ethiccraft Club conducted a seminar on the topic"Quest for Happiness" at IMS Ghaziabad on 20th August 2019. The event comprised of welcome address, felicitation of guests and a guest talk by Mr. Robin Batra, Co-Founder JBS Technologies. In the talk, sir very systematically elucidated various principles which are important for leading a happy and purposeful life. The talk was appreciated by the students present in the program and they urged for such seminars at regular intervals for the benefit of the youth.
