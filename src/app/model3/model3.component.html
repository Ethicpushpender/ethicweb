SEMINAR ON ANTI-ADDICTION

Event – SEMINAR ON ANTI-ADDICTION
Date – 27th March 2019
Venue – Auditorium, GL Bajaj Group of Institutions.

Understanding the urgency of anti-addiction in order to help the country become addiction free, Ethiccraft Club launched it’s anti-addiction campaign in the month of February. In an attempt to connect the new students with the campaign, Ethiccraft Club conducted a seminar on “Anti-Addiction” at L Bajaj Group of Institutions, Greater Noida. The seminar, conducted on 27th March helped the students know the need of such campaign in the present era. The seminar was delivered by Mr Anuj Saxena, Advocate Supreme Court and senior faculty member of Ethiccraft Club. The program was attended by a total of 130 students. After the program, the sir also met with various faculties of the institution. They all expressed their gratitude to the club for such a selfless campaign.
