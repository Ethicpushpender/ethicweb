ETHICCRAFT CLUB LAUNCH & WORKSHOP ON STRESS MANAGEMENT AT DME


Event- ETHICCRAFT CLUB LAUNCH & WORKSHOP ON STRESS MANAGEMENT AT DME
Date – 21st October, 2019
Venue – Nelson Mandela Auditorium, Delhi Metropolitan Education.

On 21st October 2019, Ethiccraft Club successfully organized a workshop on Stress Management for DME Noida, IP University students. The workshop was a part of official launch ceremony of Ethiccraft Club at the institution. The workshop was delivered by Shivanshu Singh Sir, Vice-President Ethiccraft. The workshop was attended by a total of around 120 students. The event started with a welcome address by Dr. Ravi Kant Swami, director of the institution. This was followed by felicitation of Chief Guest Vice-President Sir and Ms. Puja Saxena, Senior Executive Member of Ethiccraft Club. After this, Shivanshu Sir shared some wonderful principles in relation to stress management. After the seminar, the participants of Udgaar festival were presented participation certificates on the occasion by Director Sir, Vice-President Sir and Puja Saxena Mam. On the same day, Ethiccraft Club got officiated as a part of DME after signing the MoU with the institution. At the end of the workshop, Director of the Institution was highly satisfied and appreciated the session of Vice-President Sir and efforts of Ethiccraft Club in the society.
