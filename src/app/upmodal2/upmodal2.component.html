Personality Development Course

Ethiccraft Club is coming up with a 9 week course to enhance the personality of youth and model their thoughts, words and action. The course entitled" Personality Development Course(PDC)" aims to help youth cultivate individual, relationships and organisational values and ethics for developing professional competence. The course aims to blend character and competence in a pragmatic way to help youth embark on the path of holistic development. This 9 week certified course will be conducted at the college level inside the institution.
