ETHICCRAFT EXECUTIVE COMMITTEE MEMBER

Executive Committee Member (EEO)

Execution Committee is meant to prepare the layout and structure of the overall planning and ensure the execution of policies at ground level. Execution Committee with a team of thoughtful and selfless youths is expected to help the college volunteers of Ethiccraft Club if they face any difficulties in the implementation. Team has been created with the sincere desire of helping people march towards values and ensure the holistic development of the society.

Becoming Execution Committee Members

Responsibilities

  • Planning layout and structure of the policies and ensuring timely implementation.
  • Ensuring regular meeting and gathering of club members
  • Training the college volunteers in leadership and management.
  • Ensuring regular activities inside college campus.

Execution Committee Standards

1.Selfless Service Spirit—The committee members must be imbued with a selfless service spirit and a sincere desire to bring about a positive transformation. This is of topmost importance.

2.Inspirational leadership—Committee members are required to be exemplary in their dealings and behaviour in order to inspire people and infuse a spirit of service with dedication and joy.

3.Professionalism with dedication—The members are expected that they exhibit a strong sense of responsibility towards completion of the tasks in hand and complete them on the stipulated deadline.

4.Care and Counselling—The Execution Committee members are a natural leaders for people of different colleges therefore they are expected to care for the Needs, Interests, Concerns and Expectation(NICE) of them and provide personalized mentorship to the students in their crucial phase of life.
