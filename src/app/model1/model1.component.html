ETHICCRAFT CLUB LAUNCH

Event – ETHICCRAFT CLUB LAUNCH
Series Date – April 5, 2019
Venue – Auditorium, RKGIT Ghaziabad.

Ethiccraft Club achieved one more milestone when by the efforts of its sincere members in the institution, Raj Kumar Goel Institute of Technology, Ghaziabad gave permission to create separately Ethiccraft as a value awareness society of the institute.

On 5th April 2019, Grand inauguration ceremony of Ethiccraft society of RKGIT had been conducted in which Shivanshu Singh Sir, Vice-President of the Club was invited as Chief Guest. The whole program comprised of events including lamp lighting, felicitations, address by Director Sir and HODs about the importance of values. This was followed by a seminar on the topic” Art of Decision Making” by Shivanshu Singh Sir who enlightened the students about various principles which could empower them master the art of decision-making. Along with him, Puja Saxena Mam also attended the ceremony as Senior Executive Member of the Club. A large number of student enjoyed the seminar and want to conduct more similar sessions in the institute. The students also expressed their gratitude to the Club for taking this initiative towards youth Empowerment.
