ABOUT US

Ethiccraft Club, officially launched by Former HRD minister Mr Prakash Javadekar on 14th October 2018 at IGI Stadium, is a youth wing of ISKCON for the purpose of empowering the youth of India on the matter of values and social well-being. With the purpose of youth empowerment through knowledge blended with wisdom while simultaneously providing them with a much-needed support system and favourable ambience, a team of selfless and hardworking youths have come up with the idea of Ethiccraft Club.

Understanding the importance of values, which unlike the foundation of a building, is the foundation of human existence, the club aims to help youths cultivate know about values and subsequently help embellish their personality by cultivating values to experience the real joyful nature of the self. The club also aims to help youth provide a platform where they can express the inherent goodness of their heart by becoming instrumental in transforming the lives of others.

MISSION & VISION

VISION

To make the youth thoughtful towards importance of values in day to day life and subsequently train and inspire them to become perfect leader in the society by inculcating values, so that they can create a better India and become an asset to the nation.

MISSION

To create, preserve and promote the Value Awareness among youth through a systematic education and training program at various levels in the society which will eventually result into holistic growth heralding a constructive transformation of the hearts in order to ensure sustainable peace and happiness all over the world.

OBJECTIVES

Platform for Expressing Goodness

All of us have the inherent goodness in our personality. Many times,due to lack of a proper platform for expression and lack ofinspiration, goodness is put on the back foot. This club is aplatform for goodness expression.

Promote Value Centered lifestyle

Values are the priceless jewels which embellish the personality of a human being. Values provide the much-needed meaning to life therefore every act directed towards adding even an iota of value in the life is a transformational effort.

Association

Association has a very important role in shaping the character and destiny of an individual. This is because of the fact that association is a channel for knowledge transmission which results in thoughtful discussions subsequently leading to hear transformation

Support System

In order to become a constructive personality, along with knowledge a person need support and favourable ambience. Such ambience and support become the driving force to help one embark on the path of success.

Personalized Mentorship

Every individual has his own set of needs, interests and concerns which result in different sets of permutations and combinations of goals that one desires to achieve and obstacles which obstruct his path of success. For handling such situation, one needs personalized guidance and mentorship.

Community Building

Due to lack of training and ego-centric attitude, modern people find it easy to work as an individual but not as a team. Regular value based activities and discussions help people come together for a common cause.

Preserving Culture

Education through cultural rehabilitation (creating an atmosphere to facilitate natural and standard system of value education.

Sense of Responsibility

Training and education to make youth responsible citizens of the country. Responsible citizens are the intangible assets of the country which help the nation excel in all facets of development.

Providing Life Equilibrium

A perfect balance of Physical Quotient, Intelligence Quotient, Emotional quotient and spiritual quotient is the much needed pre-requisite for a healthy life in the modern times

Role Model and Inspirational Leadership

Creating exemplary youth leaders for the society who may proliferate the development of new frontiers to create new opportunities and avenues.

MESSAGE FROM PRESIDENT'S DESK

The 21st century is dedicated to India. It can’t be questioned that today entire world is engaging attention towards India to see what the country with a plethora of youth can do for the world. Education plays a key role in developing any nation. To educate the youth should be the prime concern of every Nation. Once DR. APJ Abdul Kalam quoted” Be more dedicated to making solid achievements than in running after swift and synthetic happiness”.
The aim of true happiness and prosperity with solid achievements is the goal of humanity from time immemorial. Right from the dawn of human civilization, the importance of Value Education has been realized and emphasized time and again by pioneers and thinkers all over the world. A positive change in the world can only be brought about through value-based education. Thus, there is a need for imparting a perfect blend of knowledge, skills and values to proliferate the rate of growth and development of an individual. Along with knowledge, youths must be empowered with wisdom to help them embark on the path of holistic development. Moreover, to nourish them in their sapling stage, along with education we must bless them with much-needed support and a favourable ambience.
Ethiccraft club is providing a platform to the modern youth who many times due to lack of proper guidance fall into the trap of bad habits. The club aims to make youth thoughtful of values in life and subsequently train them in cultivating values to help embark on the path of becoming an asset to the country. We are taking steps to build a new India where youth live a value-centred life and making them aware of our prestigious culture which unfortunately is not a part of the present education system.
It is the verdict of the intelligent class of people that the purpose of life should be a life of purpose. Through proper channels and with the help of active selfless volunteers, we are associating with youths to provide the necessary support and assistance to make them thoughtful towards the purpose of human life and become more responsible towards society.
Today, an increased number of crimes in the society itself is evidence of a lack of good leadership and role models in society. We are putting our efforts to provide a life of equilibrium where youth grow through life not just go through life. We are providing a platform to youths where they can get the proper support system to express themselves and subsequently build a community which selflessly join hands to root out the problems youths are facing today. This mission has been undertaken with the sincere desire to help the society which is currently being tormented by all kinds of problems including suicides, drug addiction, juvenile delinquency etc. May the Almighty Lord shower his blessings and together we bring peace and harmony in society and preserve our lost culture of caring and giving. With this positive note, let us all become instrumental in exhibiting that vigour, enthusiasm and determination which even has the potential to melt the rocks. When a combination of youths come together with this desire, the vision to make the world a beautiful place to live remains no more a distant dream.

Sundar Gopal Das
President, Ethiccraft
