SEMINAR ON ANTI-ADDICTION


Event- SEMINAR ON ANTI-ADDICTION
Date – 18th March 2019
Venue – Auditorium, Maharaja Surajmal Institute of Technology.

On 18th March 2019 Ethiccraft Club, launched by honorable HRD Minister Shri Prakash Javadekar with motto “Marching towards Values” aims to proliferate knowledge, skill and values among the youths organized a seminar on the De- Addiction for the students of both MSI and MSIT Janakpuri, Delhi. This event was free and open for all students.This event was held at MSIT auditorium from 12:30pm – 2pm. Dr. Pradeep Sangwan Head of Department (ECE MSIT) evening along with Rahul Janghu from ECE department who also represents Ethiccraft club at MSIT volunteered to organize this seminar to create awareness among students. This event marked the presence of 150+ students along with staff members and HODs of various departments. The keynote speaker for the seminar was Mr. Gunjan Chaudhary (M.Tech IIT Delhi, EX-IES, Faculty -IES Made Easy).

Addiction is the absolute dependency on a particular substance or situation. The problem in our society with addiction is that it is treated like a choice than as a major problem faced by an individual. Mr. Gunjan Chaudhary spoke about a variety of issues like addiction of phone, drugs, alcohol and many more which impacted all students and created awareness amongst youth to live a healthy life and not become a pray to substance addiction. Post seminar students were given the opportunity to inquire and discuss about various queries on addiction and how can one come out of these self-destructive habits. During this discussion participants were given background information about the casue and effect of addiction. This discussion lasted about 20 minutes. Students also interacted personally with Mr. Gunjan Chaudhary and Shivanshu Singh to inquire personal questions. Participants in the session reached the consensus that De-addiction is the need of time and discussed about related barriers and solutions
