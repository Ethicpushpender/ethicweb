DE-ADDICTION CAMPAIGN | PRIZE DISTRIBUTION CEREMONY

Event – PRIZE DISTRIBUTION CEREMONY
Date – 30th March 2019
Venue – Auditorium, ISKCON East of Kailash.
Participants:- 150+

“When Competition turns into contribution, everything eventually becomes a celebration.” To celebrate the contribution of youth in the Anti-addiction campaign to make India addiction free, Ethiccraft Club organized the much-awaited Prize Distribution Ceremony of Anti-Addiction Campaign. The program was organized on 30th March 2019 at Auditorium of ISKCON Temple, Delhi.

The program started with a welcome address by Mr Shivanshu Singh, Vice-President, Ethiccraft. This was followed by a drama on the theme of Anti-addiction by Parinde Theatre Group where they reiterated the urgency of spreading awareness about addictions among youth. Next was the welcome of dignitaries which included Shri Pankaj Kumar(Air India-Regional Director North India and MD, Hotel Corporation of India) and Shri Sanjay Kumar(Joint Secretary, HRD Ministry).

After this, the dignitaries awarded gifts to Campus Ambassadors of different institutions for their wonderful efforts in making the campaign successful. This was followed by an inspirational presentation about the vision, mission and opportunities for contribution by HG Sundar Gopal Das, President Ethiccraft and HG Ashraya Krishna Das(Senior preacher, ISKCON). Next was a presentation on club membership by Vice-President Sir and distribution of gifts to the topper of each institution. In the last part of the program, the students interacted with team members of Ethiccraft Club and relished delicious lunch.
