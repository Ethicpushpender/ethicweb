FOOD DISTURBUTION-AAHARAM-JIVANSYA ADHARAM


Event- AAHARAM-JIVANSYA ADHARAM
Date – September 22nd,2019
Venue – Ghaziabad.

Close to a billion people, one- eighth of the world’s population still lives in hunger. Each year 2 million children die due to malnutrition. This is happening at a time when doctors are warning of the spread of Obesity. We are eating too much while others STARVE.

As an initiation to this starvation issue our Rkgit team ETHICCRAFT conducted an Event"AAHARAM-JIVANSYA ADHARAM" on September 22,2019 at our RKGIT NGO 'Light De Literacy' which has poor and needy children. In this event volunteers distributed Halwa, Chole and Chawal to the poor children and also to their families. This little contribution of volunteers made them very happy and cheerful.This was one of the greatest feelings knowing that we as individuals can make a positive difference to them. Team Ethiccraft Rkgit
