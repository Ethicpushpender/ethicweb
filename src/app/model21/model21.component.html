TREE PLANTATION & NATIONAL SANITATION MISSION


Event- TREE PLANTATION & NATIONAL SANITATION MISSION
Date – September 29, 2019
Venue –Event in three villages of Ghaziabad.

Ethiccraft Club of RKGIT, AKGEC and ABES college in collaboration with UNNAT BHARAT (An initiative by GOI) organized a "TREE PLANTATION & NATIONAL SANITATION MISSION" event in three villages of Ghaziabad on 29 September, 2019 in which around 80 volunteers participated. In this event villagers were made aware about the dangerous effects of single use plastic and its consequences. Moreover, textile bags were distributed to people and volunteers also planted trees at various places. Plants were provided by RKGIT college and bags were given by UNNAT BHARAT. After doing this great & hard work they took love feast lunch to their utmost satisfaction at ISKCON GHAZIABAD. Students were feeling really blissful and excited. News paper covering cutting of the event will be published soon.
