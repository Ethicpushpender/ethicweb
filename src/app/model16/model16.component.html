ORIENTATION PROGRAM AT RKGIT


Event- ORIENTATION PROGRAM AT RKGIT
Date – 21st August, 2019
Venue – National Institute of Food Technology Entrepreneurship and Management.

With the sincere efforts of the volunteers and members of the institution, Ethiccraft Club conducted an Orientation Program for newly joined students of under-graduates courses at National Institute of Food Technology Entrepreneurship and Management on 21st August, 2019. The event was a part of the fortnight long orientation program conducted by the NIFTEM Campus. The event witnessed two seminars by the Club. The first seminar on the topic" Power of Habits" was delivered by Shivanshu Singh Sir, Vice-President of Club. In the seminar, Sir very systematically explained the vicious cycle of addictions through examples, case-studies and stories. In the second part, a seminar on "Developing Human Values" portraying the importance of cultivating was delivered by Mr. Raja Kumar, Deputy-Manager Maruti Suzuki. The event saw a footfall of more than 200 students and many of them even registered for upcoming Udgaar festival.
