INTRA-COLLEGE ANTI-ADDICTION QUIZ


Event- INTRA-COLLEGE ANTI-ADDICTION QUIZ
Date – Feb 2019 to Sep 2019
Venue – College campus.

As part of its initiative towards social upliftment, Ethiccraft Club launched Anti-Addiction Campaign in the month of February 2019. Under the campaign, Intra-College Anti-addiction quiz was conducted inside the institutions across India. The objectives of the quiz were as follows:
• To make an endeavor for addiction free society through education and awareness among youth to help them embark towards holistic development.
• To help youth come together for a fight against self-destructive habits.
• To make the youth thoughtful towards importance of addiction free society and help them identify their role in achieving the aforementioned objective. Many colleges participated in the quiz including IIT Delhi, NIT Delhi, JNU, RKGIT, ABES, and so on. Students could register themselves for the intra-College Quiz through Campus Ambassador (The Student representative of the club) or by Ethiccraft Website. Intra College Anti-Addiction Campaign Quiz was conducted in the college campus. Questions of both the quiz were based on the content of the book” Weed Out” written by Toshan Nimai Das. Top three Scorers were rewarded with JBL TIIOBT Pure Bass Wireless in Ear Headphone (1st Prize), X-jock Lap Bag 26.5 L Backpack (2nd Prize) and Mi MDZ-28-DI Bluetooth Speaker (3rd prize) respectively for their achievement. Top scorers of intra college Quiz who cleared the overall cut-off (decided by the quiz management) got a chance to represent the respective college in Inter-College Anti-Addiction Campaign Quiz Contest in UDGAAR Fest in the month of October at Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium. Winners and Runner-ups of the Inter-College Quiz were awarded with Honda Activa 5G DLX (1st PRIZE), Acer Spin SP315-51(2nd Prize) and Redmi Note 6 Pro (3rd Prize) and the College Authorities were honored on Stage in front of esteemed guest and 12000 youths.
