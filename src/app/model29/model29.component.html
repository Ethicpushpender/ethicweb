DISASTER WELFARE AT PATNA FLOOD AFFECTED AREAS


Event- DISASTER WELFARE AT PATNA FLOOD AFFECTED AREAS
Date – 2nd October, 2019
Venue – Patna Bihar.

Ethiccraft Club members are working on the causes like
◆Education & Training
◆Value Awareness
◆Disaster Welfare
◆Environment Protection
And many more....
Ethiccraft Club is delighted to share on 2nd October 2019, some of our members of NIT Patna Ethiccraft Club did selfless service by distributing food items to the people who were really in search of help at the flood affected areas of Patna. The event witnessed a great enthusiasm from all the volunteers in order to extend a helping hand to the people in this distressful situation Team Ethiccraft would like to congratulate the team lead by Vijay Sir and Kunal Sir including Suman Sourav, Praveen, Harsh, Chitran, Shaurya and Shushrit. Also,team would like to thank Rajswi, Naveen and Ritesh for collecting funds for this noble cause.
