AN IDEA CAN CHANGE YOUR LIFE


Event- AN IDEA CAN CHANGE YOUR LIFE
Date – 30th August 2019
Venue – GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management, Greater Noida.

Ethiccraft club successfully organised a seminar on the topic "an idea can change your life" at GL Bajaj Institute of Technology and Management, Greater Noida on 30th August 2019. The talk was given by Mr. Shivanshu Singh, Vice-President, Ethiccraft. In the talk, sir very systematically delineated some of the key principles for successful implementation of ideas.

After the program, Sir and other team members met with Director Sir and invited him for the UDGAAR festival.
