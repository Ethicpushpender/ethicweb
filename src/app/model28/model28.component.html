WORKSHOP ON STRESS MANAGEMENT SKILLS


Event- WORKSHOP ON STRESS MANAGEMENT SKILLS
Date – 22nd October, 2019
Venue – Auditorium, Ral Lal Anand College, Delhi University.

In a follow up program of the conference on Stress Management at Delhi University, Ethiccraft Club organized one workshop on Stress Management Skills at Ram Lal Anand College, South Campus Delhi University on 22nd October, 2019. The workshop was delivered by Anuj Saxena Sir, Adv Supreme Court of India and Shivanshu Singh Sir, Vice-president, Ethiccraft. In the workshop, Anuj Sir delineated mentioned some valuable principles in dealing with stress and leading a happy life. In the second part, Shivanshu Sir told some practical tips for coping up with stress. Principal sir and Rita Jain madam, overall coordinator of the workshop also attended the whole program with some other faculty members.
