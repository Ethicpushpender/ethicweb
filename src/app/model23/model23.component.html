LIVE INTER LEVEL QUIZ


Event- LIVE INTER LEVEL QUIZ
Date – 6th October, 2019
Venue – IGI Stadium.

Growing menace of addictions is a cause of great concern for the society. The problem is so gruesome that if it is not tackled timely, it will gradually devour the future of the country. Understanding the urgency of the situation, Ethiccraft Club took this concern as a project and launched “Anti-Addiction Campaign” with the purpose of imparting awareness about self-destructive addictions. Under this campaign, at first level Intra-College Anti-addiction Quiz was conducted in institutions across India and top 3 scores of each institution were awarded prizes along with participation certificates to all the participants. At the second level, top 6 students among all the toppers were selected for Inter-college Quiz at Udgaar festival. The quiz was an intellectual battle among toppers locking horns to prove their awareness on the subject of addictions. The participants of Inter-college quiz were as follows:
1. Bablu----G.B. Pant Engineering College
2. Shruti Mishra—Daulat ram College
3.Radha Pandey----Raj Kumar Goel Institute of Technology, Ghaziabad
4. Harsh---Lal Bahadur Shastri Institute of Management
5. Md. Faizan---Campus Law Centre, DU
6. Nikhil---J.C. Bose University, Faridabad
The quiz was hosted by RJ Ginnie, the famous RJ from Radio City, official radio partner of Udgaar festival. The quiz saw a tough competition among all participants and after completion of two rounds, the second runner-up prize was secured by Radha . The first runnerPandey up prize was secured by Shruti Mishra of Daulat Ram College and Bablu from G.B. Pant Engineering College emerged as the winner of the Inter-College quiz to secure a Honda Activa Scooty. All the winners were awarded on stage by Dr. Harshvardhan, Minister of Health and Family Welfare who was the Chief Guest on the occasion.
