VALUES IN ACTION

While being empowered by necessary values in one’s life, youth becomes inspired to distribute the values for the benefit of others. This phase aims to increase youth participation in the value distribution activities and make them instrumental in the attempt of adding an iota of value in the life of people.

Values in Action Phase Topics

  • Great role of great soul
  • Missionary Spirit-Working for the welfare of others
  • Lifestyle of a role-model Teacher
  • Let’s make People Better
  • Selfless motives for a Higher Cause
  • Modelling Thoughts and Actions
  • Rekindling Wisdom, Reviving love
