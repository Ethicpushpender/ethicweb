Event- ETHICCRAFT CLUB LAUNCH
Date – 14 Oct 2018
Venue – Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium.

ETHICCRAFT CLUB LAUNCH 14th Oct 2018 admin Recent Comments Off on Ethiccraft Club Launch In an initiative to boost youth empowerment with wisdom, Union Human Resource Development (HRD) Minister Mr. Prakash Javadekar launched Ethiccraft Club in the Annual Mega Youth Festival “Udgaar-An Expression of Goodness and Joy” Conducted by ISKCON Youth Forum on 14th October 2018. The festival with the theme of “Building India upon Values” was conducted at Indira Gandhi Indoor Stadium which witnessed a participation of 7000 youths from all across India. The Club with the motto “Marching towards Values” aims to sensitize and train university and school students in cultivation of values and help them become instrumental in transforming the lives of others.
