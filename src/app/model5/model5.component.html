ART OF DECISION MAKING


Event- ART OF DECISION MAKING
Date – 12th Feb, 2019
Venue – Seminar Hall.

With the vision of Pan-India expansion of Ethiccraft Club to help youths get connected with the activities of the club, Ethiccraft Club conducted a seminar on “Art of Decision Making” at Sri Ram Swaroop Memorial College of Engineering and Management, Lucknow. The seminar was conducted on 12th February 2019 at the seminar hall of the institution. Various principles governing the process of decision making were very eloquently covered by Mr Shivanshu Singh Sir, Vice-President Ethiccraft. The seminar was attended by a total of 350 students. After the seminar, the students got an opportunity to interact with team members of the Club which helped them to know more about the upcoming activities of the Club.
